The Center for Assistive Technology Act Data Assistance (CATADA) is a project funded under grant # 90AN0001 by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Administration For Community Living (ACL). CATADA provides technical assistance and supports to State Assistive Technology (AT) Programs funded under Section 4 of the Assistive Technology Act of 1998, as amended (P.L. 108-364). CATADA is a collaborative project of the Institute for Community Inclusion (ICI) at UMass Boston and The Illinois Assistive Technology Project (IATP). The information on this website does not necessarily reflect the position or policy of ACL, and no official endorsement should be inferred.
